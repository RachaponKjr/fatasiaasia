# 4. Creating an Account

## 4.1 Why Register?

With an account you can:
- ✨ Book tours
- ❤️ Save favorites to wishlist
- 📋 View booking history
- ⚡ Faster checkout
- 📧 Receive exclusive offers

## 4.2 Registration Steps

1. Click "Register" in the navigation
2. Enter your details:
   - First Name
   - Last Name
   - Email Address
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
3. Click "Register"
4. Check your email for verification code
5. Enter the 6-digit code
6. Your account is ready!

## 4.3 Verification Code

**Important:**
- Code expires in **5 minutes**
- Check your spam folder
- You have **5 attempts**
- Request new code if needed

## 4.4 Login

1. Click "Login" in the navigation
2. Enter your email
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Login"

## 4.5 Forgot Password

1. Click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email
3. Check your inbox
4. Click the reset link (valid 1 hour)
5. Enter new password
6. Login with new password

# 5. Booking a Tour

## 5.1 Selecting a Tour

1. Browse or search for your ideal tour
2. Click on the tour card
3. Review all details
4. Click "Book Now"

## 5.2 Booking Form

Fill in the required information:

### Travel Dates
- **Start Date** - When you want to begin (must be future date)
- **Visit Time** - Preferred time for pickup/meetup

### Travelers
- **Adults (18+)** - Number of adult travelers
- **Children (6-17)** - Number of children
- **Infants (0-5)** - Number of infants

**Note:** At least one traveler required.

### Contact Information
- **Email** - For booking confirmation
- **First Name** - Lead traveler name
- **Last Name** - Lead traveler surname
- **Phone** - Contact number
- **Address** - Optional

## 5.3 Submitting Your Booking

1. Review all information
2. Agree to terms and conditions
3. Click "Submit Booking"
4. See confirmation message
5. Check email for details

## 5.4 What Happens Next

After you submit:

1. **Booking Received** ✅
   - Your booking is saved
   - Status: "Pending"
   
2. **Admin Review** 🔍
   - Our team reviews your request
   - We check availability
   
3. **Confirmation & Payment** 💳
   - We confirm your booking
   - You receive payment link via email
   
4. **Payment Completed** 🎉
   - Pay securely online
   - Receive final confirmation
   
5. **Ready to Travel!** ✈️
   - Full details sent to you
   - Prepare for your adventure

# 10. Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### Q: Do I need an account to browse tours?
**A:** No, you can browse all tours without an account. However, you need an account to book tours and save favorites.

### Q: How do I get the best prices?
**A:** Subscribe to our newsletter for exclusive deals and promotions!

### Q: Can I book for someone else?
**A:** Yes, you can enter different contact details during booking.

## Booking Questions

### Q: How far in advance should I book?
**A:** We recommend booking at least 2 weeks in advance for availability.

### Q: Can I modify my booking?
**A:** Please contact us directly to modify your booking.

### Q: What if I need to cancel?
**A:** You can cancel from your profile page. Refund policy depends on timing.

### Q: How do I know my booking is confirmed?
**A:** You'll receive an email confirmation and can check status in your profile.

## Payment Questions

### Q: When do I pay?
**A:** After our team confirms your booking and sends you a payment link.

### Q: Is my payment secure?
**A:** Yes, we use Omise, a trusted payment processor with bank-level security.

### Q: Can I pay in installments?
**A:** Currently we require full payment. Contact us for special arrangements.

### Q: What currency do you charge in?
**A:** Prices are shown in USD but converted to your local currency at checkout.

## Account Questions

### Q: How do I change my password?
**A:** Go to Account Settings and click "Change Password."

### Q: I didn't receive my verification code
**A:** Check your spam folder. If not there, click "Resend Code."

### Q: Can I delete my account?
**A:** Please contact customer support to request account deletion.
